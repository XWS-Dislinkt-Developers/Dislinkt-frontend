  About Dislinkt
Our goal is to create a business social network to connect employers and job seekers.
Participants/users of the system and general opportunities for them:
  • Non-registered user - Search and browse public profile posts.
  • Administrator - Has full access and control of the system.
  • Non-registered user - Search and view posts, upload own posts , search and create job offers, correspond with contacts and followers.
